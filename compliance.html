Research Use Only

All peptides sold by AMINENT LABS are designated For Research Use Only (RUO). They are not intended for human consumption, therapeutic use, veterinary use, or diagnostic applications. These products are intended solely for in vitro research and laboratory experimentation conducted by qualified professionals.

Not for Human Consumption

AMINENT LABS peptides have not been evaluated or approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) or any equivalent regulatory body for use in humans or animals. Purchasing, possessing, or using these peptides for purposes other than legitimate scientific research may violate local, state, or federal regulations.

Purchaser Responsibility

By purchasing from AMINENT LABS, the buyer acknowledges and agrees that:

  • All products will be used exclusively for legitimate research purposes.
  • Products will not be administered to humans or animals.
  • Products will not be resold or redistributed for non-research purposes.
  • The purchaser is responsible for compliance with all applicable laws and regulations in their jurisdiction.
  • The purchaser has the appropriate qualifications, facilities, and institutional approvals to handle research chemicals.

Regulatory Status

AMINENT LABS peptides are classified as research chemicals. They are not pharmaceutical products, dietary supplements, food additives, or cosmetic ingredients. The classification and regulatory requirements for research chemicals may vary by jurisdiction.

It is the purchaser's responsibility to ensure that their use of these peptides complies with all applicable institutional, local, state, and federal regulations.

Product Labeling

All AMINENT LABS products are clearly labeled with the following designations:
