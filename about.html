About AMINENT LABS

A research peptide supplier built on the principle that quality and transparency are inseparable.

Our Mission

Advancing research through reliable peptides

AMINENT LABS was founded with a clear purpose: to provide the research community with peptides they can trust. We understand that the integrity of research depends on the integrity of materials, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Every peptide in our catalog is manufactured under controlled conditions, tested using validated analytical methods, and documented with comprehensive certificates of analysis. We believe researchers deserve complete transparency about the materials they work with.

Principles

What Guides Us

Quality First

We never compromise on purity or testing standards. Every peptide meets our minimum 98% purity threshold before it reaches a researcher.

Full Transparency

Complete analytical documentation accompanies every order. We provide the data so researchers can verify quality independently.

Research Integrity

We serve the scientific community. Our compliance standards and research-use-only designation reflect our commitment to responsible supply.

Our Approach

From synthesis to shipping

Our peptides are produced using solid-phase peptide synthesis and purified through preparative HPLC. After synthesis, each batch undergoes a multi-step quality control process including analytical HPLC, mass spectrometry, and amino acid analysis.

We work with independent third-party laboratories to provide additional verification, ensuring that our internal results are confirmed by unbiased external analysis.

Peptides are packaged in sealed vials under controlled conditions and shipped with appropriate cold-chain protocols when required. Every order includes complete documentation for traceability and compliance.
